Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University Visual Intelligent Center (VIC), supervised by Prof. Jingyi Yu. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in 2020 from ShanghaiTech University.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision and graphics, including neural rendering and 4D reconstruction.
